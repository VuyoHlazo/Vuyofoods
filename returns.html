Returns & Refunds Policy

At Buddy9Nine Deliveries, we value your satisfaction and strive to provide high-quality products, including fresh braai meat and beverages. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we handle returns and refunds carefully to ensure fairness and safety for all customers.

1. Fresh Food & Alcohol

Our braai meat and alcoholic beverages are prepared and packaged for immediate consumption. Once delivered, we cannot accept returns of these items due to hygiene and safety regulations.

2. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, spoiled, or incorrect item, please contact us within 2 hours of delivery with a photo and a brief description of the issue. We will review each case individually and offer:

  • A replacement item delivered as quickly as possible, or
  • A partial refund if replacement is not feasible, or
  • A full refund for incorrect or spoiled items, at our discretion.

3. Order Accuracy & Responsibility

Please ensure your order details, delivery address, and contact number are correct when placing your order. We are not responsible for issues arising from incorrect information provided by the customer.
